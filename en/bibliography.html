4 Livres qui pourrait vous plaire

1967

“Grave of the Fireflies” by Akiyuki Nosaka

A novella about the survival of a brother and sister in wartime Japan.

1982

[Image] “Nausicaä of the Valley of the Wind, Volume 1” by Hayao Miyazaki

A manga that is a must-read for all science fiction fans. With an epic, ecological, and humanist story, it is a precursor to Miyazaki's work.

1989

“Howl's Moving Castle” by Diana Wynne Jones

A novel based on which the Ghibli film was made. It is full of magic, flying castles, and fascinating characters.

1993

“Earthsea, Book 3: The Farthest Shore” by Ursula K. Le Guin

A novel about a coming-of-age journey that explores themes of growth, responsibility, and balance.
